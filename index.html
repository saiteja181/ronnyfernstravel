WHO WE ARE

Founded by Ronaldo Fernandes back in 2019 November, the name of the company derived is from the short form of his name (Ronny Ferns). An entrepreneur at heart, it was our founders drive and passion to do something different in the travel industry that led to the start of this company. Initially started with the vision, to make tourism in Goa, safe and cost effective for all tourists both national and international. It has now become our mission to make goa a tech-based travel destination over coming years.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Here Are Most Customers Asked About Us.

  • What services does Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment offer?

    Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment offers a wide range of services including flight bookings, hotel reservations, car rentals, tour packages, event ticketing, and travel insurance.

  • How can I book a flight with Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment?

    You can book a flight with Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment online through their website, by phone, or in-person at one of their offices. Simply select your desired destination and travel dates, and choose from the available flights and airlines.

  • Does Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment offer travel insurance?

    Yes, Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment offers travel insurance to protect against unexpected events such as trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. They offer a range of insurance options to suit different needs and budgets.

  • Can I customize a tour package with Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment?

    Yes, Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment offers customizable tour packages to suit individual preferences and budgets. You can choose from a variety of destinations, activities, and accommodations to create your ideal itinerary.

  • How can I cancel or modify my booking with Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment?

    If you need to cancel or modify your booking with Ronny Ferns Travel and Entertainment, you can do so by contacting their customer service team via phone or email. Depending on the terms and conditions of your booking, there may be fees or restrictions involved.
